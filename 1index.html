Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer same-day service? +
Same-day repairs are available when parts are in stock! For software issues like virus removal and tune-ups, we can usually complete the work the same day. For hardware repairs requiring parts, we'll keep you informed every step of the way.
What areas do you serve for mobile repair? +
We primarily serve Chapel Hill, Pittsboro, Carrboro, and surrounding areas in the Triangle. Contact us to confirm we serve your location.
Do you backup my data before repairs? +
We offer optional data backup solutions before repairs to protect your files! Ask about our Permanent Backup Drive for long-term file safety. We can backup your important documents, photos, and files before any repair work begins for peace of mind.
Do you provide warranties on repairs? +
Yes! We provide warranties on both parts and labor. The specific warranty period depends on the type of repair, but we stand behind all our work. Hardware repairs typically come with a 90-day warranty.
What brands of laptops do you repair? +
We repair all major laptop and PC brands including Dell, HP, Lenovo, Asus, Acer, Samsung, and more. We work on both Windows laptops and desktop PCs.
How much does hinge repair cost? +
Hinge repair typically costs $100-$200 depending on the laptop model. Broken hinges can damage your screen and motherboard if not fixed, so it's important to address them quickly.
How long does a typical repair take? +
Software repairs typically take 2-4 hours. Hardware repairs depend on parts availability but usually 1-3 days. We'll give you a time estimate when we diagnose your device.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, checks, PayPal, Venmo, and Cashapp for your convenience.
