Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day service? +
Same-day repairs are available when parts are in stock! For software issues like virus removal and tune-ups, we can usually complete the work the same day. For hardware repairs requiring parts, we'll keep you informed every step of the way.
What areas do you serve for mobile repair? +
We primarily serve Chapel Hill, Pittsboro, Carrboro, and surrounding areas in the Triangle. Contact us to confirm we serve your location.
Do you provide warranties on repairs? +
Yes! We provide warranties on both parts and labor. The specific warranty period depends on the type of repair, but we stand behind all our work.
Can you recover data from a dead hard drive? +
In many cases, yes. We can recover data from failed hard drives and SSDs. The success rate depends on the type and severity of the failure. We'll evaluate your drive and provide an honest assessment.
What brands of laptops do you repair? +
We repair all major brands including Dell, HP, Lenovo, Asus, Acer, Samsung, and more. We work on both laptops and PCs.
How long does a typical repair take? +
Software repairs typically take 2-4 hours. Hardware repairs depend on parts availability but usually 3-5 days. We'll give you a time estimate when we diagnose your device.
Do I need to back up my data before service? +
We recommend backing up when possible. While we take precautions to protect your data, it's always best to have a backup. We can help you create one if needed.
Do you backup my data before repairs? +
We offer optional data backup solutions before repairs to protect your files! Ask about our Permanent Backup Drive for long-term file safety. We can backup your important documents, photos, and files before any repair work begins for peace of mind.
Do you repair broken hinges? +
Yes! Hinge repair typically costs $100-$200 depending on the model. Broken hinges can damage your screen and motherboard if not fixed, so it's important to address them quickly.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, checks, Venmo, and PayPal for your convenience.
